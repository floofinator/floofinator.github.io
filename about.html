About Me

Bio

I am Luke Thomson, but I also go by Floofinator on the internet!

From a young age I have loved technical concepts and complex mechanisms. Seeing a system work in a well designed manner is very satisfying to me. I started out being very technically minded, but overtime I have grown to love expressing ideas by creating art and stories.

Beyond living in places, I have also travelled to the United Kingdom, Ireland, and Canada. One of my goals in life is to visit and appreciate the many different parts of the world and understand their cultures.

I am a Californian born, Australian lived, currently Florida residing individual. My early life involved much moving around and living in different places. Because of this I have been to many different schools and made connections with many different people and areas of the world.

Interests and Goals

My interests in general revolve around the use of technical skills for creative works. I am constantly learning how technical mediums can be used to teach and entertain.

I wish to work on genuine projects with a passionate team who can make things into a reality. I enjoy creating immersive worlds and experiences to be viewed and interacted with. My hope is to prove that video games can be an impactful medium for art.
