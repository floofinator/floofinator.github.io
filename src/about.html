Bio

I am Luke Thomson, but I also go by 'Floofinator' on the internet.

This alias intends to evoke my passions. 'Floof' evokes a sense of playful softness and thoughtfulness, while the suffix '-inator' conveys the power to create through design and technical strength like the evil geniuses before me.

My admiration for technical systems leads me to learn endlessly on how to manifest them into being. Seeing a system work in a well designed manner is very satisfying to me and the prospect that I can achieve the same drives me. I grew up very technically minded, but overtime I grew to dream of expressing ideas through creating art and stories. I now know that the best way to achieve this is for me to combine these thoughts into a well designed mess.

My early life involved a decent quantity of moves around the globe. I am a Californian born, Australian lived, currently Florida residing individual. Because of this I have been to many different schools and made connections with many different people and areas of the world.

One of my goals in life is to visit and appreciate the many different parts of the world and understand their cultures. Fortunately for me, I have travelled to the United Kingdom, Ireland, Canada, Italy, France, Monaco, The Vatican, and Spain. The internet has helped in my open worldview greatly and I will continue to connect with as wide a range of people as I can.

Interests and Goals

My interests in general revolve around the use of technical skills for creative works. I am constantly learning how technical mediums can be used to teach and entertain.

I wish to work on genuine projects with a passionate team who can make things into a reality. I enjoy creating immersive worlds and experiences to be viewed and interacted with. My hope is to prove that video games can be an impactful medium for art.
